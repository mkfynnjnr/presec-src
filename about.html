About PRESEC Legon

Presbyterian Boys' Secondary School (PRESEC) is a historic secondary educational institution in Ghana, located in Legon, Accra. Founded in 1938, PRESEC has become one of the most prestigious and academically excellent schools in Ghana.

The school is known for its outstanding academic achievements, including winning the National Science and Maths Quiz (NSMQ) multiple times. PRESEC has produced numerous leaders, professionals, and change-makers who have contributed significantly to Ghana and the world.

The school's motto, "MONOVICIT QUI IUSTE LABORAT" (He Conquers Who Labours Justly), reflects the institution's commitment to excellence, integrity, and hard work.
